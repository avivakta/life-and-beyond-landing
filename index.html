How it works

Step 1

Set Up

Lay out the board, place pins, and seed the tax fund. Everyone starts equal — choices change everything.

Step 2

Play & Negotiate

Buy, build, and negotiate. Use knowledge of the rules to find legal edges and creative plays.

Step 3

Evaluate Outcomes

Count assets and impact. Who grew value, who stabilized the “economy,” and who only looked rich?
